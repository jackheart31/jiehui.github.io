Kobe Bryant

Basketball Legend

Here's a time line of Bryant's life:

  • 1978 - Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
  • 1996 - Bryant becomes the youngest NBA player in history at 18 years old.
  • 1998 - Bryant becomes the youngest starter in his first NBA All-Star Game.
  • 2000 - Bryant wins his first NBA title against Indiana Pacers in six games.
  • 2002 - Bryant wins his first All-Star Game MVP. He proceeds to win it 3 more time during the rest of his career.
  • 2008 - Bryant wins a gold medal at the Beijing Olympics. He wins it again in 2012 at London.
  • 2014 - With 32,310 points, Bryant overtook Michael Jordan to become third on NBA's all time scoring list.
  • 2016 - Kobe Bryant retires, scoring 60 of 101 points in his final game against Utah Jazz
  • 2020 - Kobe Bryant died in a helicopter crash along with his 13 year old daughter Gianna "Gigi" Bryant.

"I don't want to be the next Michael Jordan, I only want to be Kobe Bryant."

-- Kobe Bryant
