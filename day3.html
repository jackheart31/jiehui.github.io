Romance Train to Gangchon Rail Bike Station, 2.5km

From Gangchon Rail Bike Station, there is a free shuttle 
bus service about 5 mins walk away, departing 5-10 mins 
after getting off from the Romance Train to bring visitors 
back to departure station, Gimyujeong Rail Bike Station
